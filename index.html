About

Two worlds,
one maker.

I'm Smriti, a Harvard student with a special concentration in Computational Human Mechanics, focused on wearable technology. I spend my days where the body meets the machine: building firmware, analyzing biosignals, and designing devices that live on human skin.

But I've always worked with my hands in a different way too. I founded a campus tailoring service, taught myself garment construction, and debuted two original fashion collections exploring what it means to dress between two cultures.

For me, engineering and fashion ask the same question: how do you make something that fits?
